Random variables

Randomness

  • A random process is a definite outcome that cannot be predicted with certainty

    • Coin flip

    • Student test scores (for example, your EC2203 mark)

    • Waiting time in the College shop

    • The FTSE100 index

    • Salary in your first job

  • We use random variables to analyze random processes

Random variables

  • Provides a numerical summary of a random outcome

  • A random variable assigns a real number to every possible outcome of a random process

  • A random variable takes different values depending on the outcomes of the random process under study
